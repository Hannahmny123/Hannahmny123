Welcome to HANmade!

HANmade is an e-commerce platform that specializes in offering a wide range of handmade products crafted with passion and creativity. The goal is to provide customers with unique and high-quality items that are carefully crafted by yours truly.

About the Owner



I am Hannah Mae Baran Manaya, a 3rd year student of Caraga State University, taking up Bachelor of Science in Information System.
Crafting has been my passion since I was young, and it has become not only a hobby but also a source of income for me as a student.

I find joy in creating handmade items, and through HANmade, I aim to share this joy with others by offering unique, carefully crafted products. Each piece in our collection represents dedication, creativity, and craftsmanship.
